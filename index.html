About Game
To Move the Spaceship: ASDW / Arrow Keys
To Shoot: Space / Left-Mouse-Click
  • To Increase the Score: Collecting Coins gives you +5 Score. Killing aliens gives you +5 Score. Killing aliens of higher strength gives you the score accordingly.
  • When Aliens Die: Some Aliens release Powerups when they die, collect them to get bonus points. Some Aliens releases Fire when they die, escape from it.
  • Gun Strength: The number of bullets released from the gun at a time is decided by your gun strength. As the level increses, you'll bydefault get higher gun strength.
    Level 4 or higher: Gun Strength - 2
    Level 7 or higher: Gun Strength - 3
    P.S. Maximum Gun Strength - 5
  • Player Health: At the top of game, a green-coloured bar is shown which represents spaceship health. Colliding with Enemies reduces your health. Collect Health Portions to increase your health.
  • Special Levels: Special levels helps to boost your score. They are red-coloured aliens which are much stronger than the usuals so they come with thier health bar.
